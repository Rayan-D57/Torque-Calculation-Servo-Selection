
Torque Calculation & Servo Selection

1. Basic Equation:
   τ = F × r
   Where:
   - Force (F) = m × g
     - m = 1 kg
     - g = 9.81 m/s^2
     - F = 1 × 9.81 = 9.81 N
   - Arm lengths:
     - r1 = 4 cm = 0.04 m
     - r2 = 10 cm = 0.10 m
     - r3 = 15 cm = 0.15 m

2. Torque Calculation for Each Joint:
   - Joint 3:
     τ3 = F × r3 = 9.81 × 0.15 = 1.4715 N.m
   - Joint 2:
     τ2 = F × r2 = 9.81 × 0.10 = 0.981 N.m
   - Joint 1:
     τ1 = F × r1 = 9.81 × 0.04 = 0.3924 N.m

3. Safety Factor:
   Safety Factor = 1.5
   - Joint 3:
     τ3 = 1.4715 × 1.5 = 2.20725 N.m
   - Joint 2:
     τ2 = 0.981 × 1.5 = 1.4715 N.m
   - Joint 1:
     τ1 = 0.3924 × 1.5 = 0.5886 N.m
